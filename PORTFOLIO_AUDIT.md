## STEP 7 — Case Study Rewrite Framework (Use for every project)
Use this exact six-panel structure:

1. **Context:** market + user pain in one sentence.
2. **Constraint:** what made this hard (time, budget, data, complexity).
3. **Thesis:** your strategic decision.
4. **Build:** architecture + key implementation calls.
5. **Proof:** metrics, outcomes, behavioral change.
6. **Leverage:** why this matters for the next company hiring you.

Avoid “I made X using Y.”
Lead with **business movement** and **decision quality**.
